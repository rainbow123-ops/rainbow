Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is invented by medieval Europeans making carrot based puddings. Carrot cake is a baked frosted dessert being distinct from older European carrot puddings. Carrot cake originates during the Middle Ages in Europe. When sugar is costly, rationed carrots, are used for sweetness and have high sugar content. Carrot cake is made differently across the world. In Brazil, this cake is moist, bright orange, and airy where the carrots are puréed rather than grated.

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon of ground clove
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots

Frosting:

  • Coffee
  • 4 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. You must first preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Second, you must mix the oil, brown sugar and granulated sugar.
  3. Next, you must add the eggs, one at a time and the vanilla extract.
  5. Next, you must alternate mixing in the carrots and the dry ingredients.
  6. Now, you must place the cake in the oven.
  7. While the cake is baking in the oven, you can work on the frosting.
  8. In a stand mixer with the paddle attachment you must put in the room, temperature, butter, and cream cheese.
  9. Then you must set the mixer on low speed.
  10. Next, you can add the powdered sugars, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  11. After, you can get the cake out of the oven and let it cool.
  12. Now you can frost it and you can enjoy the cake.
